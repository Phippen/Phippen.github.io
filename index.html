Voice-a-Sketch

This project was developed during HackISU in the fall of 2015. My involvement in this project was focused on implementing the features of Google Polymer and researching voice recognition.

Intro to Psychology

Intro to Psychology covered many topics that I believe were very useful not just for my career choice but for life in general. How humans develop and how they think at different ages, biases that people tend to have when making decisions or obtaining new information, and the effects of being anonymous are a few that I think were particularly important to learn when working with clients and users as a software engineer.
